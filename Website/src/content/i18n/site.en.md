# Carbone Stellaire

The carbon in my blades comes from the heart of stars. It is born through the triple-alpha reaction, scattered across the universe by supernovas, and eventually finds its way into steel. Carbone Stellaire is a tribute to that raw, powerful origin. I hand-forge each blade in my workshop, then give every knife its own character. Browse the ranges below, or contact me for a custom project.

# Crafting Process

Making a knife by hand requires control over a full chain of craft skills. It starts with steel: choosing the right alloy for the intended use, forging the blade by hand, and keeping the hammered texture as a visible signature. Heat treatment then gives the edge its durability and precision. Finally, the handle must extend the blade naturally, both in shape and material. A handmade knife is useful, beautiful, and built to last.

# The Workshop

Carbone Stellaire is a bladesmithing and knife-making workshop in the north of the Cotentin peninsula.

My name is Thibault Wittemberg. I have been designing handmade knives since early 2023, shaping each blade in my own gas forge. Every piece is designed to balance efficiency, durability, and visual identity.

I make three main ranges: Edge Of Space, Cyber Edge, and Le Sciotot, along with SciFi Legends pieces inspired by science-fiction icons.

I also take custom requests. Use the contact form if you want to discuss a specific piece.

# Edge Of Space

The Edge Of Space is an elegant table knife, both modern and incisive, with sharp facets and a dropping blade while keeping a timeless line through its slender, curved handle.

It comes as both a fixed knife and a folding knife.

I designed it as the knife that would be issued to every newly graduated astronaut explorer: both a symbol of the ancient tools that could save a life during an exploration mission, and an example of human craft.

# Le Sciotot

Le Sciotot comes in four versions:

- a small all-purpose kitchen or utility knife
- a full kitchen knife in its Longboard edition
- an oyster knife
- a summer pendant shaped like a surfboard

All are handmade, and their steel is fully forged by hand.

The shapes recall a surfboard as a tribute to the remarkable Sciotot beach in north Cotentin.

# Cyber Edge

The Cyber Edge is a knife that can be used in the kitchen or at the table. Its design is sharp and angular. The grip is comfortable thanks to a handle with a wide return at the end.

I imagined it as a knife from a dystopian future, made on a cyberpunk planet colonized by humankind to relocate its industrial activity.

# SciFi Legends

This collection brings together exceptional knives inspired by iconic science-fiction sagas.

Every major sci-fi franchise has its own codes, aesthetic, and colors. They instantly identify their worlds: the uniforms in Star Trek, lightsabers in Star Wars, worms in Dune, the jungle in Predator, or the xenomorph in Alien.

This collection gathers handmade knife creations that pay tribute to the universes and distant worlds that inspire me every day.
